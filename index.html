What is the MycoVerse?
The MyCoverse is a non-profit database that stores different types of
mushrooms, classified by their properties such as: consumable, unsafe, medicinal, etc.

Basics of a Mushroom

What is a mushroom? Mushroom are the fleshy, spore-bearing fruiting body of a
fungus, typically produced above ground on soil or another food source. These
mushrooms grow from underground mycelium and can expand rapidly under favourable
conditions, with its properties varying depending on the species. Some mushrooms
are beneficial and can either be used for medicine or simply be consume, while other
types could be psychotic or toxic to humans.
